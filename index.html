Who are we?


At University Admissions tutors, we are experts in the application process for Oxford and Cambridge. Our tutors are greatly experienced working with candidates from all backgrounds and know exactly what the mistakes are which applicants make and how to avoid them. Our mission is to empower students to reach their full potential, and we are passionate about helping our students get into their dream university.

What do we do?


Our programmes are comprehensive and guide students through every stage of the application process, to ensure that they are completely prepared for the admissions procedure. We are experienced in giving personalised tutoring for personal statements, entrance exams and mock interviews. Beyond this, we offer pre-application mentoring programs for further academic development, allowing students to push beyond the school syllabus.
